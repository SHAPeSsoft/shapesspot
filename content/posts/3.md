# Recently,
I was bored out of my mind, so I decided **"Hey... I wonder what will happen if I curled grok.com's ping.txt** So I did, I ran:

```bash
curl grok.com/ping.txt
```

And I got back this HUGE wall of HTML, JSON, CSS all the stuff. Well it turns out Grok's whole IOS/Android/Car app with the voice stuff actually has descriptions and prompts for them! And they are hilarious, I don't know who Xai hired as prompt engineers, but they were feeling... **creative**.
