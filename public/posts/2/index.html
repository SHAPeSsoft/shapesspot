Vibe Coding...#

It’s a… subject thats for sure. I’m going to just come out and say Vibe Coding != Software Engineering. That is fact, you saying something and then doing a little Ctrl+C/Ctrl+V action isn’t programming, it’s prompting, and I mean REALLY prompting, endless amounts of times. Over. And. Over. Now I now you probably don’t care what I have to say about, but you should! Honestly, a buggy, insecure app that breaks when you try to add a new button because ChatGPT/Grok/Claude/Gemini/whateverthefuck forgot what it was doing or thought it’s a exotic bird is WAY better than a app that you spent time, effort, and (maybe) passion into that actual, which works more than a AI app! Just learn to program. That’s all I gotta say, stop hyping AI like it’s gonna make you a buncha money NVIDIA on the other hand.... Though, I do think using AI as a tool and not another brain or arm is great! Work smarter not harder everyone (granted that you know what the “smart” is!).
